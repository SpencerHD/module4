Location

7467 Gratiot Road

Saginaw, MI 48609

(989) 439-1128


At Coco Loco Mexican Grill and Bar on Gratiot Road, you will get the chance to experience the flavors of Mexico with authentic dishes prepared fresh when you order. Our menu features a large variety of Mexican cuisine – from Nachos and Salads to Quesadillas and Enchiladas. Our specialty dishes include Cancun Grill Mix, Ceviche Trio, Shrimp Enchiladas, and much more! Our Fajitas are served hot to straight to your table! Available for dine-in, or carry-out, our menu is sure to have something to satisfy your cravings!? You can enjoy authentic Mexican meals, traditional surroundings, and a commitment to service that goes above and beyond your expectations. We are located just west of Tittabawassee River on Gratiot Rd. Find us near Roberts Community Park and Swan Valley High School.
