About Us

At Coco Loco Mexican Bar & Grill, we bring the authentic Mexican food experience to you. With our hacienda styled restaurants, all of our inside decor is hand painted in traditional Mexico style, where not only our food is part of the culture. We have three locations in the Saginaw and Bay City areas, and welcome you to visit us and try our outstanding flavors for all of your favorite foods!
